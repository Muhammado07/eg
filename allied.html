Allied

Allied products

Only offered exclusively by Al Salem Johnson Controls, the Allied Products division brings together supporting products & projects capable of completing the HVAC&R systems for our nationwide customer demands & services.

Al Salem Johnson Controls emphasizes on providing its customers with complete solutions for (HVAC/R), by offering a wide range of products to complement their air conditioning systems. These products include an assortment of air conditioning components from worldwide famous brands that share in our high standards. We are pleased to offer industrial and commercial Ventilation Fans from YORK (USA), Chilled Water Pumps, Expansion Tanks & Air Separators by TACO (USA), Central Water Heaters by Bradford White (USA), Variable Air Volume Boxes, Smoke & Fire Dampers from Ruskin-Titus (USA/UAE), and many other products and accessories. In fact, Al Salem Johnson Controls can provide all your (HVAC/R) requirements from a single source.

At Al Salem Johnson Controls, we truly understand HVAC systems. In fact, we produce some of the most robust HVAC equipment, parts and controls on the market today. But we don’t just sell products. We also offer the expertise, services and strategic insight to make them work within your current system, optimizing your building’s performance and lowering your overall costs. If you’re interested in revolutionizing your HVAC system, contact an expert today.
