Replacement Parts

Replacement Parts

Maintaining the performance of your AC and HVAC equipment depends on the quality of its Replacement Parts. Al Salem Johnson Controls supplies YORK® original equipment manufacturer (OEM) replacement parts that ensure that your equipment will operate at maximum efficiency.

Our Replacement Parts Centers has a team of qualified and knowledgeable staff to assist you in purchasing the correct part and to avoid inducing loss entirely. At the same time, our service teams are ready to complete the repair in order to get you up and running in as little time as possible.

Following Replacement Parts are available at our Showrooms and Dealers:

  • Residential AC Products
  • Light Commercial AC and HVAC
  • Commercial HVAC
  • Refrigeration – Sabroe, Frick, Gram, Stal
  • Controls – Thermostat, Valves
